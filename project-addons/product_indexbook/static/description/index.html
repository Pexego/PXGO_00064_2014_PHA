Product Indexbook for Odoo

Product Identification Sheets for Quality Control

Features:
1. Assignment of species and macroscopic characters to products for QC purposes
2. Automatic creation of P.I.S. for products lots

Notes:
This module activates "lots and serial numbers" and "multiple locations" warehouse options
You need to create/activate a warehouse location as quarantine location and check "Has P.I.S.?" option in those products that needs this feature
When a product with "Has P.I.S.?" option checked is transferred to a quarantine location, this module automatically creates his associated P.I.S.
